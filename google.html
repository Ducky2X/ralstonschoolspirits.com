School Spirit

Ralston High School offers a lot of things like sports, clubs, and partnerships. Some of these clubs include DECA, Art Club and Yearbook. Some of the sports include Basketball, Football, and Soccer. They also hold very fun events for students and teachers. These events include the kickball tournament, school plays, and the volleyball tournament we had. Ralston also holds special events that create big moments for students like Homecoming and Prom. Ralston has around 1,000 students that go to the school. We also have a few websites such as Ralston Schools and RKids. The people that authoritize this school include the principal, Ms. Tvrdy and the vice principal, Dr. Pivonka. Ralston High School was created in 1954.
